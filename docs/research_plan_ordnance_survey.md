# Ordnance Survey Street Data Research Plan

## Research Objectives
Research OS open data and commercial datasets for complete London street information, focusing on:
1. OS OpenData products (AddressBase, CodePoint Open, Meridian 2)
2. OS licensing and access methods
3. Free vs paid dataset comparison
4. Street name extraction by postcode area for SE London (SE1-SE18)
