# UK Government Data Sources Research Plan

## Task Overview
Research and identify official UK government and authoritative data sources for:
1. Complete UK postcode database with post town mappings (SE2 Abbey Wood, SE3 Blackheath format)
2. Complete list of UK counties and local authority areas  
3. Official UK street names database with comprehensive street coverage

## Target Sources
- ONS (Office for National Statistics)
- Ordnance Survey
- Postcode Address File (PAF)
- Other government sources

## Research Focus Areas
- Data completeness and coverage
- Update frequencies and data currency
- Download formats and access methods
- Licensing restrictions and costs
- Government vs commercial availability
- Technical specifications and schemas

## Success Criteria
- Identify official sources for all three data types
- Document complete access and licensing information
- Provide technical specifications for each source
- Create actionable recommendations for data acquisition
